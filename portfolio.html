Let's play a game! Bioshock Trivia

Make your way through a list of questions only a true BioShock fan will be able to answer. This particular app is pretty simple, but it's still my favorite. Make sure your sound is on! Bonus cool points if you get a perfect score.


This app was built solo.

Let's talk about movies! Movies with Friends

This is a fun little prototype app designed to connect people with similar taste in films. The app offers a forum of movie data pulled in from Reddit, as well as suggested friends who have at lease one favorite movie in common.


This app was built as part of a two man team.

Let's relax a little! Zen Den

This app was inspired by my own experience with anxiety. Anxiety affects 1 in 5 americans and is highly manageable with the proper tools. I was tired of having to use three apps to manage my anxiety, so I put a group together to build a better app.


This app was created with a four man group.
